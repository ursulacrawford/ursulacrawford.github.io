About Me

Hello, I’m Ursula Crawford – a stylist with a passion for creating timeless and expressive looks. My work spans editorial, commercial, and personal styling, with a focus on clean silhouettes, color play, and unique textures that bring each story to life. Whether it’s on set or one-on-one, my goal is to elevate every client’s vision through style.

Rooted in both classic fashion foundations and forward-thinking aesthetics, I bring a refined but approachable perspective to every project. I believe in storytelling through wardrobe and the transformative power of personal style.
